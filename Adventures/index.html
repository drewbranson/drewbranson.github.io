Valemount Extended weekend

Long weekend in August, did hike on June 29, 2024. Alpine lakes were still ice covered. Meadow had yellow and white flowers but the lilies were still several weeks from blooming

Three day weekend:

  • Friday after work: drive to rec site nearby
  • Saturday: Drive to trailhead and hike up to meadow, find level place in meadow to set up camp, hang out by alpine lakes, explore mountain
  • Sunday: Hike down mountain, hang out on rec site, walk along lake
  • Monday: Pack up, drive back to the Loops
