Places to Visit in Oregon

Portland

Explore the vibrant city known for its food scene, microbreweries, and Powell's City of Books. Visit the International Rose Test Garden and the Portland Japanese Garden.

Cannon Beach

Marvel at the iconic Haystack Rock and enjoy the scenic coastline with stunning views. Take a walk along the sandy shores and visit the art galleries in town.

Columbia River Gorge

Drive along the Historic Columbia River Highway and stop at Multnomah Falls, Bridal Veil Falls, and Vista House for breathtaking views of the gorge.

Mount Hood

Head east of Portland to see the majestic Mount Hood. Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, skiing, and snowboarding. Visit Timberline Lodge for its rustic charm.

Bend

Located in Central Oregon, Bend offers outdoor adventures, including hiking, mountain biking, and river activities. Don't miss visiting the nearby Newberry National Volcanic Monument.

Crater Lake National Park

Witness the stunning blue waters of Crater Lake, the deepest lake in the United States. Take a scenic drive around the rim or hike some of the park's trails.

Oregon Coast

Explore the picturesque coastal towns such as Newport, Florence, and Astoria. Enjoy beachcombing, whale watching, and visiting charming lighthouses along the way.

Ashland

Known for the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, Ashland offers world-class theater performances. Stroll through Lithia Park and explore the charming downtown area.

Painted Hills

Discover the vibrant hues of the Painted Hills in the John Day Fossil Beds National Monument. Take short hikes and learn about the area's geological history.

Crater Lake National Forest

Experience the diverse landscapes of this national forest, featuring volcanic peaks, lush forests, and cascading waterfalls. Don't miss the Umpqua Hot Springs.

Silver Falls State Park

Enjoy a hike through the park's Trail of Ten Falls and witness the stunning waterfalls, including the popular South Falls and North Falls.

Oregon Dunes National Recreation Area

Explore the unique sand dunes along the Oregon coast. Try sandboarding or take a dune buggy tour for an adrenaline-filled experience.
